The Streets & Drainage Division provides services such as inspecting and repairing roadways and alleys, managing flood prevention efforts, and maintaining ditches and stormwater pipe systems. They also construct and repair sidewalks, curbs, and gutters, and respond to potholes and surface issues through patching, milling, and emergency street repairs.

Street Maintenance

The Street Maintenance Branch is responsible for inspecting and restoring damaged roadways and maintaining alleyways. This is achieved through the mill & overlay method, which provides an improved driving surface for residents. Additionally, this branch collaborates closely with other city departments to support long-term infrastructure planning and ensure cohesive development across the city.

Street Patching

The Street Patching Branch specializes in restoring road surfaces by patching potholes, milling worn pavement, and applying fresh asphalt. They promptly respond to emergency repairs to ensure streets remain safe and drivable, even after unexpected damage. Additionally, this branch is responsible for performing patching following BPUB utility repairs, ensuring seamless and durable road surfaces.

Drainage

The Drainage Branch is dedicated to flood prevention through the maintenance of city ditches and storm drain systems that channel runoff during heavy rain. Additionally, they provide construction services for minor drainage improvement projects designed in-house. Their efforts help protect homes, businesses, and infrastructure from water-related damage, ensuring the community remains safe and resilient during adverse weather conditions.

Concrete

The Concrete Branch is responsible for building and repairing sidewalks, curbs, and gutters throughout Brownsville. Their work promotes pedestrian safety, enhances accessibility, and efficiently guides stormwater, supporting clean and walkable neighborhoods across the city. Additionally, this branch undertakes unique projects from multiple departments that require expert concrete craftsmanship. These projects include constructing concrete slabs of any size or specification.
