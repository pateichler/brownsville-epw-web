Street Drainage

Dedicated to flood prevention and stormwater control, this branch maintains city ditches and oversees pipe systems that channel runoff during heavy rain. Their efforts help protect homes, businesses, and infrastructure from water-related damage.

Concrete

The Concrete Branch builds and repairs sidewalks, curbs, and gutters across Brownsville. Their work promotes pedestrian safety, enhances accessibility, and helps guide stormwater efficiently, supporting clean, walkable neighborhoods throughout the city.

Street Patching

This team specializes in restoring road surfaces by patching potholes, milling worn pavement, and applying fresh asphalt. They also respond to emergency repairs to ensure streets remain safe and drivable, even after unexpected damage.
