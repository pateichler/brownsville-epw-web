Description about Greens and Hot Shot.

Greens

The Greens staff is comprised of two Supervisor, one Forester and thirty (30) full-time employees. The staff is responsible for the grounds maintenance on City owned and leased buildings as well as other designated locations throughout the City. The Greens division is responsible for many duties which includes the mowing, edging and trimming of trees, watering trees and emergency tree removals. 

  • Evaluated and trimmed 3,465 trees
  • Removed 675 damaged or diseased trees
  • Watered 10,772 trees
  • Mowed 3,568 sites in Brownsville
  • Planted or relocated 282 trees
  • Mowed 160 miles of ditches 
  • Scraped 39 miles of curbs 
  • Swept 10,827 miles of streets
