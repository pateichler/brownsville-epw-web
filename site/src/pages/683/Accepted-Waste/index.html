Accepted Household / Residential Waste


  • Normal Household Waste
  • Old Furniture and Bedding
  • Refrigerators and A/C
    Including written certification stating that all refrigerant has been evacuated.
  • Stoves
  • Washers/Dryers
  • Yard Waste (grass, leaves, branches, trunks)

Accepted Construction Waste


Regular Landfill waste fees will apply to any renovation, construction, or demolition waste which would include:
  • Brick
  • Carpeting
  • Concrete
  • Doors
  • Insulation
  • Old Tool Sheds
  • Paneling
  • Plywood Sheets or Remnants
  • Roof Shingles
  • Sheetrock
  • Stairs
  • Tar-Paper
  • Windows
  • Wood and Chain Link
  • Wood Framing
Note: There will be no exceptions to these items.
