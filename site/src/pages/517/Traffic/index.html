Traffic signs, traffic signals, and parking are all integral to the safety of motorists and pedestrians.

Brownsville's Traffic Department provides the city with essential services with its 25 full-time employees. This department keeps Brownsville residents and visitors traveling smoothly and efficiently on local roadways. The Department's primary function is to provide traffic safety to the public and is divided into three Unique Divisions.

Traffic Signals

The Traffic Signals Division is responsible for maintaining and repairing all traffic signal lights throughout the city. This includes ensuring that signals are working safely and efficiently to help drivers, pedestrians, and cyclists navigate intersections. The team also handles upgrades, timing adjustments, and emergency repairs as needed.

Traffic Signs/Markings

The Signs and Markings Division manages all roadway signs and pavement markings. This includes installing and maintaining stop signs, street name signs, and directional signs, as well as painting lane lines, crosswalks, and other street markings. Their work helps keep our streets safe, organized, and easy to understand for everyone using them.

Motor Vehicle Parking System

The Motor Vehicle Parking System Division oversees the operation and maintenance of public parking in downtown Brownsville. This includes parking meters, signage, and other infrastructure that supports safe and accessible vehicle parking. The team works to keep parking efficient, fair, and functional for residents, businesses, and visitors.
